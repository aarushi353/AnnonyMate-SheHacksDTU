What We Do

Annonymate is the easy to use safe space that gives you confidential support of your qualified and experienced school counsellor when you need them.A counsellor will help you share your emotions,analyse your feelings and make sense of the thoughts that trigger them.They listen to you intently and help you excel personally and proffesionally.With guidance and practice tools,you will feel happier,stronger and grow at every step.

We Help With

Anonymate is a Counselling platform specially built
for school students that helps students excel in their
life by dealing with day to day life stresses stemming
from Relationships, Stress, Low Confidence, Anxiety,
etc over live chat and face to face interaction with
their school counsellor who is well acquainted with
all the school related problems and knows how to
tackle mental issues. They can freely communicate
with the counsellor without worrying about their
confidentiality

Thrive

Students tackle with self-doubts and fear of failure from time to time. Counselling will help you to tame your inner critic and break patterns of self-defeating thoughts and limiting beliefs.We at anonyMate aim to solve all the problems faced by them by providing student a platform where they can easily register and communicate with their school counsellor without worrying about their confidentiality.
