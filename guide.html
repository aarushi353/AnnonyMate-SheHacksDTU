Guide For Students


1. Sign up with the registered user id (admission number) and a pin provided by your school.
2. Upon signing up, you will be asked to change your user id and pin so as to keep your identity confidential.
3. When you require help you can freely sign in using the user id and pin generated by you during sign up process.
4. Point to be discussed - In case you forget your pin you need to click on forget password. You can easily create a new pin by giving your used id.
5. As soon as you sign in you can easily chat with your school counsellor.
6. Counsellor will be available from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm
7. You can also adjust the time with your counsellor on the chat.
8. In case of any issue feel free to contact us.

Guide For Counsellor


1. Sign up with the registered user id (admission number) and a pin provided by your School.
2. Upon signing up, you will be asked to change your user id and pin
3. Counsellors are requested to sign in using the newly generated used id and pin every day and stay signed in from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm.
4. In case of special cases, they can collaborate with their patients and arrange a different time slot according to their convenience.
5. If the counsellor is not present during the specified time, he/she has to inform us via email so the stand by counsellor can be arranged.
6. In case the counsellor is not logged in on time then our team will send an email to the school.
7. In case the counsellor finds the situation that requires the help of Parent’s he need to write to us so that our legal team can discuss the matter and come to a valid solution. If our team is convinced then only the parents will be involved
8. In case of any issue, you are free to contact us via email or the contact number available 24*7.

Guide For School


1. School is requested to register using name, the email id and password.
2. As soon as you register our team with reach out to you via email.
3. Each School is required to provide the two counsellors.
4. One permanent counsellor and a stand by counsellor that is required to stay online and signed in from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm
5. School needs to provide the database of all the students consisting of the User Id, Parent’s name, Contact Details of Parents, and a unique pin generated by them for each student.
6. In case of any issue, you are free to contact us via email or the contact number available 24*7.
